Events

Discord events are triggers that are fired when specific actions or occurrences happen in a Discord server, in order to use an event you must enable it here.

Show Warning

    All events will be unlocked if you enable this option.


    It's generally recommended to selectively enable only the events that are essential for your bot's functionality. This approach not only safeguards user privacy but also enhances the overall performance of your bot by minimizing the retrieval of unnecessary event data.


    If your bot genuinely requires access to all events and its required intents, is still in development, or is not intended for public use, you may proceed with caution.

Intents

When you enable or disable events, the corresponding intents are automatically enabled to meet the requirements of the enabled event. Therefore, only modify this switches if you are confident in your understanding of intents, remember that certain events may require specific intents. Learn more.
